ABOUT THE COMPANY

JOURNY OF PRUDENT

Formed in June 2018 , we have embarked on an exciting journey of imparting quality education to students and participants.
Training software languages to over 100 students in the first year is an achievement for us.
We are into short term courses, workshops and also into placement oriented grooming.
